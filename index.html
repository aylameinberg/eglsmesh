Who - EGLS Community What - building a decentailized communication network for thr EGLS Community. Why - To How - Using LoRA technology. Join - Here are several compelling reasons to set up a neighborhood Meshtastic network:

  • Emergency Communication: Meshtastic provides reliable off-grid communication during disasters or power outages, ensuring neighbors can stay connected when traditional networks fail 1.
  • Community Resilience: By building a decentralized mesh network, communities enhance their ability to coordinate during crises, making the neighborhood more self-reliant 1.
  • Neighborhood Security: Residents can collaborate on smart security or neighborhood watch initiatives using shared LoRa-based IoT networks 2.
  • Extended Network Coverage: Strategically placing nodes (e.g., router devices) helps rebroadcast signals and improve overall network reach 3.
  • Privacy and Encryption: The network supports encrypted messaging and allows users to adjust location precision for added privacy 45.
  • Open-Source and Community-Driven: As an open-source project, Meshtastic benefits from active community support, including local groups that assist with setup and expansion 67.
